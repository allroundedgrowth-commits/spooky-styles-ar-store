# Product Overview

**Spooky Wigs** is a year-round e-commerce platform for wigs and head accessories with augmented reality try-on capabilities.

## Product Categories
- Professional wigs (work, everyday wear)
- Casual & fashion wigs
- Costume & theatrical wigs
- Head accessories (hats, headbands, clips)

## Core Features
- **AR Try-On**: Real-time face tracking with 2D/3D wig visualization using TensorFlow.js and MediaPipe
- **E-commerce**: Full shopping cart, checkout, and order management
- **Guest Checkout**: Purchase without account creation
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe and Paystack integration
- **Admin Dashboard**: Product management, analytics, inventory tracking
- **User Accounts**: Order history, profile management, saved preferences

## Brand Identity
- Halloween-inspired aesthetic (purple, orange, black color scheme)
- Spooky but professional design
- Accessible year-round, not just seasonal
- Focus on quality and user experience

## Target Users
- Costume enthusiasts
- Theater/performance professionals
- Fashion-forward individuals
- People exploring new looks
- Anyone needing wigs for medical, religious, or personal reasons
