### Step 1: Sign Up (FREE)
1. Go to https://www.meshy.ai
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
3. Use your email (no credit card needed)
4. Verify your email
5. You get 200 FREE credits immediately

### Step 2: Prepare Your Wig Images

**What you need:**
- Clear photo of the wig
- Good lighting
- Preferably on a mannequin head or model
- White/neutral background is best

**Where to get images:**
- Your own product photos
- Supplier product images
- Stock photos (with license)
- AI-generated images (Midjourney, DALL-E)

**Image tips:**
- Resolution: At least 512x512px
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Clear view of the wig
- Front-facing angle works best

### Step 3: Generate 3D Model

1. **Login to Meshy.ai**
2. **Click "Image to 3D"**
3. **Upload your wig image**
4. **Select settings:**
   - Art Style: "Realistic" (best for wigs)
   - Topology: "Quad" (better quality)
   - AI Model: "Meshy-4" (latest)
5. **Click "Generate"**
6. **Wait 2-3 minutes**

### Step 4: Download GLB File

1. Once generation is complete, click the model
2. Click "Download"
3. Select format: **GLB** (not GLTF, not FBX)
4. Save to your computer

### Step 5: Upload to S3

1. Login to your admin dashboard
2. Go to Products ‚Üí Edit Product
3. In the "3D Model URL" field:
   - Upload the GLB file to S3 (use your upload feature)
   - Or manually upload to S3 bucket
4. Copy the S3 URL
5. Paste into "Model URL" field
6. Save product

### Step 6: Test in AR

1. Go to your store
2. Click on the product
3. Click "Try in AR"
4. Allow camera access
5. See your wig in 3D!
